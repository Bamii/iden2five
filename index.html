Iden2five

A blockchain based product verification and distribution system for open and transparent exchange in agriculture.

Enter your email to be notified.

iden2five is a system built on the blockchain to transform the way products are distributed and purchased. We are using the blockchain to ensure product authenticity and constant distribution of agricultural products, thus linking the farmers to markert and assuring the consumers of high-quality products. Our goal is to ensure that agricultural products are distributed via verifiable channels to ensure authenticity, safety of consumers as well as a stable market for the farmers. Over time, we want our method to spread to other cuntries, so we can deliver effective distribution, transparency and safety in this sector.

Brief Overview

Manufacturers Blockchain

This blockchain maps ownership of property and ensures that such ownership is forever secure. It allows for identification of producers/farmers based on location and other advanced categorization.

Distributors Blockchain

This blockchain records verified, identities for distributors and distribution outlets. It makes it possible to check that a particular distrubutor actually exists, is legitimate and verified on the chain.

Consumers Chain

With properly verified identities, it enables people contact and make purchases on the chain as well as investigate product authenticity.

Open Expense Chain

This blockchain makes all expenses public and verifiable by anybody. Governments and philantropic organizations alike can use it put out aids, grants, tenders for projects, and request for bids that cannot be manipulated.
